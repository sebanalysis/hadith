Book 39. KITAB SIFAT AL-QIYAMAH WA'L JANNA WA'N-NAR
(Book giving description of the day of judgement, paradise and hell)

Chapter
  1. Description of the day of judgement, paradise and hell
  2. The beginning of the creation and the creation of Adam (peace be upon him)
  3. Pertaining to re-birth. assembling and characteristics of the earth on the day of Resurrection
  4. The feast for the inhabitants of paradise
  5. The question of a Jew from Allah's Messenger (may peace be upon him) about soul and his words: "They ask thee about the soul"
  6. Pertaining to the words of Allah: "Allah would never punish them so long as you are amongst them"
  7. Pertaining to the words of Allah: "Nay, but verily man is inordinate, because he looks upon himself as self-sufficient"
  8. The splitting up of the Moon (a great miracle)
  9. There is none to show snore patience than Allah. The Exalted and Glorious, on attributing wrong things to him
  10. The non-believers would be made to crawl on their faces
  11. Dipping of the most affluent people of the world in the hell-fire and dipping of the most (worldly) miserable in Paradise
  12. The similitude of a believer and a non-believer
  13. The believer is likened to the date-palm tree
  14. The mischief of satan in the Muslim society and sending his detachments for the creation of turmoil, and there is a devil attached to every person
  15. None would attain salvation because of his deeds but it is through Lord's Mercy (that he would attain that)
  16. The doing of good deeds very frequently and putting one's best efforts in devotion to Allah
  17. Moderation in giving sermon
