Book 21. Kitab-Us-Said Wa'L-Dhaba'Ih Wa Ma Yu'Kalu Min Al-Hayawan
(Games And Animals Which May Be Slaughtered And The Animals That Are To Be Eaten )

Chapter
  1. Hunting with the help of trained dogs
  2. When the game goes out of sight, but is later on found
  3. It is unlawful to eat fanged beasts of prey and birds with talons
  4. It is permissible to eat the animals of water even if they are dead
  5. The eating of the flesh of domestic asses is unlawful
  6. Pertaining to the eating of the flesh of the horse
  7. Pertaining to the flesh of the Lizard
  8. Permissibility of eating locusts
  9. Permissibility of eating the flesh of the hare
  10. It is permissible to make use of things necessary for hunting and chasing but the use of small pebbles is disapproved
  11. Good attitude even in slaughter and killing and sharpening of large knife
  12. It is forbidden to tie the animals (and then kill them)
  13. It is forbidden to tie the animal and make it a target of arrows
