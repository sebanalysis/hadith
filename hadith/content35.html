Book 35. KITAB AL-DHIKR
(Book pertaining to the remembrance of Allah, supplication, repentance and seeking of forgiveness)

Chapter
  1. Exhortation for the remembrance of Allah, the Exalted
  2. The names of Allah, the Exalted, and the merit of one who enumerates them
  3. Supplication should be made with conviction and will
  4. It is not something appreciable to make request for death from the Lord when one is in trouble
  5. He who loves to meet Allah, Allah also loves to meet him, and he who dislikes to meet Allah. Allah also abhors to meet him
  6. Merit of remembrance of Allah and calling Him, and nearness to Allah, the Exalted
  7. The disapproval of supplication in which a request is made to Allah for punishment of one's (misdeeds) in the world
  8. Merits of the assemblies in which Allah is remembered
  9. The merit of this (supplication) : "O Allah, grant us the good in the world and good in the hereafter and save us from the torment of hell"
  10. The merit of pronouncing Tablil, Tasbib and supplication
  11. The merit of an assembly for the recitation of the Qur'an or for the Dhikr (Remembrance of Allah)
  12. Excellence of seeking forgiveness from Allah and seeking that very much
  13. Excellence of remembering Allah in a low voice
  14. Supplication for the safeguard of one's self against the evil of the turmoil
  15. Seeking of rufuge in Allah from the evil of destiny and from the severity of calamity
  16. Supplication at the time of sleeping and going to bed
  17. Refuge from the evil of what one has done, and what one has not done
  18. The reciting of Subbin Allah in the morning and at the time of sleeping
  19. The excellence of supplicating at the time of the crowing of the cock in the morning
  20. Supplication during the time of trouble
  21. The merit of Subhan Allah Wa Bi Hamdihi
  22. Excellence of supplicating for the Muslims behind their back (in their absence)
  23. Excellence of reciting Al-Hamdu Li-Allah after eating and drinking
  24. Supplication is granted if the supplicant does not show impatience
