Book 34. KITAB AL-I'LM
(Book of Knowledge)

Chapter
  1. Prohibition of making a hot pursuit of the allegories contained in the Qur'an and avoiding those who do it, and of disputation in the Qur'an
  2. Pertaining to a disputationist
  3. Following the footsteps of the Jews and the Christians
  4. Those who indulged in hair-splitting were ruined
  5. Knowledge would be taken away and ignorance would prevail upon people and the turmoil at the end of the world
  6. Pertaining to one who introduced something good or evil or he who called to righteousness or to the path of error
